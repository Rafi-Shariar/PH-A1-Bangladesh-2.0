Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation drives progress through technology, startups, and creativity, fostering economic growth, job creation, and sustainable solutions for the future.

Sustainability

Sustainability balances development and nature by embracing renewable energy, eco-friendly practices, and green policies to protect future generations.

Infrastructure

Modern infrastructure enhances connectivity, economic growth, and inclusivity through smart cities, resilient planning, and expanded transportation and digital networks.

Empowerment

Empowerment fosters equality through education, skills, and inclusion, uplifting individuals and ensuring everyone contributes to and benefits from progress.
