Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation drives progress through technology, startups, and creativity, fostering economic growth, job creation, and sustainable solutions for the future.

Sustainability

Sustainability balances development and nature by embracing renewable energy, eco-friendly practices, and green policies to protect future generations.

Infrastructure

Modern infrastructure enhances connectivity, economic growth, and inclusivity through smart cities, resilient planning, and expanded transportation and digital networks.

Empowerment

Empowerment fosters equality through education, skills, and inclusion, uplifting individuals and ensuring everyone contributes to and benefits from progress.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Bangladesh 2.0 is a nation of possibilities, where innovation drives progress, sustainability ensures harmony with nature, and empowerment uplifts every individual. Together, we build a future where poverty is history, and opportunities are abundant for all. Our infrastructure connects dreams, and our collective spirit fuels growth. This is the Bangladesh I envision—a beacon of hope, resilience, and endless potential for the world to admire and follow."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
